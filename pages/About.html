What is NunchyCrut all about?

My name is Professor Simon P. Thinkwell, I am the creator and administrator of this site and this is my story...

In the year 1832 my great, great, great, great, great Grandfather, the late and much celebrated Pastor Rudyard Thinkwell was born.

Rev. Rudyard Thinkwell, circa 1776

Thinkwell, as you probably know, was a decorated world war veteran, treasure hunter, deep sea diver, adventurer, and of course inventor!

One of the things Rudyard is not well credited for, and he should be, is inventing what we now know as the website.

The discovery

It all started about 2 months ago when I found a dirty old sea-chest in the attic. This thing was crawling with lice and barnacle-encrusted sputum but I managed to prize it open...and what was inside was to change my life forever!

Treasure? No, even better! I found piles and piles of filthy, stinky old documents that had almost completely rotted, the text practically illegible. But after painstaking restoration work we were able to make some startling discoveries about what we now know as the internet.

The NunchyCrut headquarters, circa 1706.

Now - here is the shockng part, the internet has existed for centuries!!! That's right, we can trace it back as far as the early 1800's and my great, great, great, great, great, great grandfather was at the heart of it all back then... so he was.

Ramblings

Amid the reams of insane ramblings were tales of how the very first websites were hand-coded in cave painting format, tutorials on how to carve <div> and <a> tags out of rock and wood. We were amazed at what we found.

The writings chronicle everything from the birth of the very first pop-up to responsive layouts crafted with Spandex and Lycra. We wil learn all about Rudyard and his famous NunchyCrut web design studio in Stoke on Trent's infamous Coal Valley.

Blog

The very day I discovered these journals I immediately began compiling this information into a website where I proudly present Rudyard's writings in the form of a blog.

Rudyard's first search engine.

The blog section will provide you with transcribed extracts from my great, great, great, great, great, great...great grandafather's journals in chronological order so that you can trace the secret history of the internet!

You will be astounded by the sheer inventive genius behind concepts we new take for granted.

So read, learn and enjoy!
